Campbell's Chunky Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

Gonna preface this review by saying that I am two-thirds of a bottle of wine in right now and listening to the new Fall Out Boy album, so this might be unnecessarily dramatic. This soup taught me who I am. The meat is choice. The vegetables are there. The spice kisses you like a lover. The protein content is modest at 14g, but to quote Shakespeare: "Though she be but little, she is fierce". Plus, 80% of your daily sodium requirement prevents you from having to eat sodium for the rest of the day, an enticing convenience. Mr. Campbell outdid himself with this one. Pick one up at your local Publix today for $3.09. Or don't, I don't give a f**k. Verdict: 9/10
