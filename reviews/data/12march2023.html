Campbell's Chunky Pub-Style Chicken Pot Pie

Campbell's Chunky Pub-Style Chicken Pot Pie is a classic take on British cuisine, in that it is indeed food, but is also entirely devoid of flavor. That 16g of protein will however make it an enticing option to the gym-going populace, although those 16 grams are heavily counterbalanced by 19 grams of fat. It does have vegetables, and they are mushy and flavorless, just like I like them, so no complaints there. I do like it at this price point, I feel that it at $2.49 per can it is well positioned in the meal-replacement soup market. Overall a solid option for those who have a microwave, 3 minutes, and $2.49. Verdict: 6/10
